Biography

About Me

I am currently an Associate Professor with the School of Information Science and Technology, Southwest Jiaotong University (SWJTU), Chengdu, China. I received the Ph.D. degree (advised by Prof. Hong-Ning Dai and Prof. Chuan Chen) from the Macau University of Science and Technology (MUST), Macau, China. I received the M.S. degree (advised by Prof. Xi-Le Zhao) from the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China, Chengdu, China.

Education and Work Experience

  • 2023.07-2025.12: Lecturer, School of Information Science and Technology, Southwest Jiaotong University (SWJTU)

  • 2020-2023: Ph.D. in Faculty of Information Technology, Macau University of Science and Technology (MUST) (Supervisor: Prof. Hong-Ning Dai)

  • 2016-2019: M.S. in Applied Mathematics, University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC) (Supervisor: Prof. Xi-Le Zhao)

  • 2012-2016: B.E. in Mathematics and Applied Mathematics, Taiyuan Normal University
